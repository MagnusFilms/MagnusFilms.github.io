A Travellers Guide To Tamriel

Upon paths less traveled

From Skyrim's frosty peaks in the north down to the sandy deserts of Elsweyr, the continent of Tamriel has many amazing sights to see, towns to visit, and ruins to explore. With this comprehensive travel guide, even you could become a legendary adventurer.

Notable Destinations of Tamriel

Skyrim

The land of the Nords, filled with snow-capped mountains and hearty mead.

Morrowind

A mysterious province of ashlands and mushroom forests, Home to the Great Houses.

Elsweyr

Home of the Khajiit, where desert sands meet jungle thickets and moon sugar flows.

Hammerfell

A harsh land of red deserts and fierce Redguard warriors, ancient ruins dot the sands.

Cyrodiil

The heart of the Empire, a province of great cities, forests, and imperial ambition.
